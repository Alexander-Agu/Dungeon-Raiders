Welcome To Dungeon Raiders

To become the altimate raid master you must defeat all creatures lucking in the Dungeon.
Use the items in the shop to help you reach your goal.

How to Play.

  1. Play close attention to player and enemy stats while playing the game
  2. As you play, both the player and enemy health reduces
  3. You will get an option of 3 enemies and you can only choose to fight 1 at a time.
  4. When you have chosen an enemy 3 buttons will apear (Quit, Fight Enemy, Take Reward)
  5. If you choose to quit the game is over
  6. Press the fight enemy button multiple times until either you or the enemy is dead
  7. The shop is also available and you can buy items anytime even while fighting an enemy
  8. You will only be able to use the reward button after defeating an enemy
